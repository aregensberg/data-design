Use Case

Title: Creating a “highlight” to show off items she sells in her store. Description: Kelly wants to make an easily accessible spot for her followers/clients to view items for each season that she has already shared. Name/Role: Kelly; wants to showcase her shops new/old items in an easy place to help boost views and sales. Usage Preconditions: Kelly already uses Instagram but wants to have a recap section for users to look at the items she has posted previously to her stories. Usage Postconditions: Kelly successfully makes highlights of clothing items based on the season.

Interaction Flow:

  • Kelly opens Instagram on her chrome book by typing (www.instagram.com) and logging into her account.
  • The site directs Kelly to her “home” page displaying her feed.
  • Kelly clicks on “profile” to view her own profile.
  • The site takes Kelly to her profile which displays all her posts.
  • Kelly clicks “New” in the “Highlights” section of the page.
  • The site provides a chronological list of stories that she has posted previously.
  • Kelly chooses 7 of the recent summer clothing items she posted last weekend.
  • Kelly then clicks “Next” on the site to proceed.
  • The site allows Kelly to title the highlight and choose a cover picture.
  • Kelly names the highlight “Summer” and leaves the default cover photo to what the site choose then clicks “Done”.
  • The site directs Kelly back to her profile and she can now see the “Summer” highlight.
  • Kelly clicks “Summer” to watch her highlight and appreciate the items she is showcasing.

Frequency of use: Kelly uses highlights weekly to show off items and how to pair them.
