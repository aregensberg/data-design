Conceptual Model and Entity Relationship Diagram

Entities and Attributes

Profile:

  • profileId (primary key)
  • profileActivationToken (User Authentication)
  • profileEmail
  • profileHash (Account Password)
  • profileImage

Item: (strong)

  • itemId (primary key)
  • itemImage
  • itemDate
  • itemPrice

Stories: (strong)

  • storiesID (primary key)
  • storiesImage
  • storiesContent
  • storiesDate

Highlights: (weak)

  • hightlightsItemsId (foreign key)
  • highlightsStoriesId (foreign key)

Relationships:

  • One profile belongs to one person (1 to 1)
  • One profile can have many stories posted ( 1 to many)
  • Many stories can be turned into many highlights (many to many)
