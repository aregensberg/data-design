Conceptual Model and Entity Relationship Diagram

Entities and Attributes

Profile:(strong)

  • profileId (primary key)
  • profileActivationToken (User Authentication)
  • profileEmail
  • profileHash (Account Password)
  • profileImage

Item:(strong)

  • itemId (primary key)
  • itemImage
  • itemDate
  • itemPrice

Story:(strong)

  • storyId (primary key)
  • storyContent
  • storyDate

Highlight:(weak)

  • highLightProfileId (foreign key
  • hightlightItemsId (foreign key)
  • highlightStoryId (foreign key)

Relationships:

  • One profile can have many stories posted (1 to many)
  • One item belongs to one story (1 to 1)
  • Many stories can be turned into many highlights (many to many)
